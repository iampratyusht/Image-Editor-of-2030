  torch.hub.load("intel-isl/MiDaS", "MiDaS_small")

ADDITIONAL NOTICES:

This project uses MiDaS for monocular depth estimation to:
1. Generate depth maps from single RGB images
2. Compute surface normals for physics-based relighting
3. Provide geometric priors for the neural relighting network

The depth estimation enables realistic lighting effects by understanding
the 3D structure of the scene from a 2D image.
